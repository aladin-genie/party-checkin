🎉 Party 2026 Check-In System

Complete Workflow Guide

1
Guest Registration
Guest visits the registration page and fills out their details.
Required: Name, Email, Number of Tickets
Payment Method: Zelle / Venmo / Cash
Transaction ID: Zelle confirmation number or note
2
Payment Instructions
Guest sees payment details based on their selected method.
Zelle: Send to [YOUR_EMAIL/PHONE]
Venmo: Send to @[YOUR_HANDLE]
Cash: Pay at the door
Amount: $30 × number of tickets
3
Pending Approval
Guest sees confirmation: "Registration submitted! Pending verification."
Guest receives: Nothing yet (waiting for approval)
You receive: Notification of new pending registration
System: Stores guest info, waits for admin action
4
Admin Verification
You log into admin dashboard and verify payment in your bank app.
Go to: /admin (password protected)
Check: "Pending Approval" tab
Verify: Transaction ID matches in Zelle/Venmo app
Click: "✅ Approve & Send QR"
5
QR Code Delivery
System generates QR code and emails it to the guest automatically.
Guest receives: Email with QR code attached
QR contains: Unique check-in code
Guest should: Save QR code (screenshot or print)
Status: Ready for party check-in!
6
Party Day Check-In
Guest arrives and scans QR code at the entrance.
Guest: Opens QR code on phone
Scanner: Camera reads QR code
Audio: "Welcome [Name]! You have [X] tickets."
Staff: Hands wristbands based on ticket count
Done: Guest enters party! 🎉
